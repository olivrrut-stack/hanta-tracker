Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

HantaWatch ("the App") is operated by Oliver Rutland ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

HantaWatch does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Health or medical information about you
  • Any data that could identify you personally

2. Local Data Storage

The App stores a cache of disease outbreak data locally on your device. This data consists entirely of publicly available health information (case locations, counts, and descriptions). It contains no personal information and is stored solely to allow the App to function when you are offline. This data never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple's MapKit framework to display maps. Apple's own privacy policy governs any data processed by MapKit. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

When you tap "VIEW SOURCE" links within the App, you are directed to external websites (such as who.int or cdc.gov). Those websites have their own privacy policies which we do not control.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because we collect no information from anyone, the App is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. GDPR (European Users)

Because we collect no personal data, we do not process any personal data under the meaning of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). No lawful basis for processing is required as no processing occurs.

6. CCPA (California Users)

Because we do not collect, sell, or share personal information, California residents have no personal data to request access to, deletion of, or opt-out from the sale of.

7. Data Security

All data stored by the App is kept entirely on your device. We have no servers and no ability to access data on your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: oliver.rutland@icloud.com
