About the App

HantaWatch displays known hantavirus case locations on a global map using data sourced from public health organizations including the WHO, CDC, PAHO, and ECDC. The app is designed to raise awareness about where hantavirus outbreaks have occurred.

Data Accuracy

The case data bundled in this app reflects publicly reported information at the time of the last app update. It may not reflect the most current outbreak status. Always consult official public health sources for up-to-date information.
